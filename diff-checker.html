About the diff checker

This free diff checker (text compare tool) puts two versions of your text side by side and highlights exactly what changed, line by line — added lines in green, removed lines in red. It uses a longest-common-subsequence algorithm to find the smallest set of changes between the two texts. Everything runs client-side in your browser, so nothing you paste is uploaded to a server.
