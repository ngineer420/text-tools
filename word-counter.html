About the word counter

This free word counter updates live as you type or paste. It reports your word count, character count (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and an estimated reading time, alongside a keyword-density table of your ten most frequent words. Everything is calculated client-side in your browser, so nothing you type is ever uploaded to a server.
