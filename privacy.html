Last updated: July 2026

textkitpro.com ("we", "the site") provides free browser-based text tools: a word counter, case converter, Lorem Ipsum generator, and text diff checker. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the site.

How the tools work

All four tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Text you type or paste is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by this site. Closing the tab discards everything.

These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or generally at aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic (e.g. page views, approximate location by country, device type). This data is not linked to the content you process on the site.
